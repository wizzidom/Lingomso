    About Us

    The Company is Located and operates in Walkerville, Johannesburg with production Units also located in Walkerville.

    Our Vision

    Lead the way towards eradicating farming in Gauteng and throughout the World by improving the productivity of farmland and the nutritional value of food produced while preserving and enhancing the environment

    Mission Statement

    Attain all year round sustainable production and supply ability through enhancing small-scale farmers towards the best use of land and provision to both the consumers and small-scale farmers a reliable market of high-quality farm products.

    Our Values

    We commit to sustain, empower small farmers, and maximize land use. Integrity guides us to ensure a steady supply of quality farm goods. We value fair market access, strong farmer-consumer ties, and innovation for better farming. With eco-care and teamwork, we build a thriving farm system.

    Services

    The following range of services are offered can be offered by LINGOMSO LAMANINA Primary Cooperative

    CROP FARMING

    LINGOMSO LAMANINA Primary Cooperative owns one farm. Farm located at Walkerville district. The farm is used for crops production using various irrigation systems as they are supporting irrigation farming. Various ranges of crop are produced starting from Spinach, Tomato, Maize, Cabbage and Ginger.

    LIVESTOCK FARMING

    INGOMSO LAMANINA Primary Cooperative has variety of livestock held at company’s farms whereby Chicken make the main livestock project for the time being.
    The main products from poultry range from:
    • Chicken Eggs
    • Chicken Meat
    • Day old chicks from hatchery unit

    PRODUCTS PROCESSING & PACKING

    LINGOMSO LAMANINA Primary Cooperative tends to process farm products in the process of value addition to the farm produce, whereby:
    • Maize/corn are processed into corn flour or animal feed ingredient
    • Soybean by products are also processed into animal feed ingredients
    • Sunflowers by products are also processed into animal feeds ingredients

    FORMAL, INFORMAL TRAINING & EXTENSION SERVICES

    . FORMAL, INFORMAL TRAINING & EXTENSION SERVICES. LINGOMSO LAMANINA Primary Cooperative facilitate links to both formal and Informal training as well as extension services specifically to esteemed customers who purchase day old chicks from the company and contracted farmers. These customers are treated as potential future suppliers of eggs to the company. The training involves :
    • Chicken housing
    • Handling of day old chicks
    • Brooding
    • Feeding
    • Growth management
    • Keeping chick healthy (Vaccination, Temperature, Ventilation, Lighting etc)

    PRODUCTS MARKETING

    LINGOMSO LAMANINA Primary Cooperative invest on securing a reliable market for the products produced within the company as well as those products produced by out growers. Out-growers Management LINGOMSO LAMANINA Primary Cooperative manages a list of out growers who produces fertile eggs for hatching at the company. Logistics and crops transportation LINGOMSO LAMANINA Primary Cooperative manages the storage and transportation of crops produced upcountry into Dar es Salaam for processing and value addition.

    RANGE OF PRODUCTS AT LINGOMSO LAMANINA

    LINGOMSO LAMANINA Primary Cooperative deals with a range of various products related to crop farming and Livestock farming. Livestock farming Involves but not limited to the following:

    i. Cattle/ Dairy Cow (Products Includes Milk and Meat)
    ii. Goat (Products Includes Milk and Meat)
    iii. Chicken (Products Includes Eggs and Chicken Meat)
    iv. Pig Meat

    Crops farming Involves but not limited to the following products:

    i. Maize (Corns)
    ii. Spinach
    iii. Potatoes
    iv.Tomatoes
    v. Cabbage

    PROJECTS MANAGEMENT AT LINGOMSO LAMANINA

    The Management Team at LINGOMSO LAMANINA Primary Cooperative is made up by professional staffs and dedicated individuals with a passion for making an impact in the agriculture sector through management of viable agriculture projects and products value additional using the full-time employees and hired experts on call basis.
